Privacy Policy for LoveFace App

Last Updated: [06.25.2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how the LoveFace application ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from you:

a) Information You Provide Directly to Us:

  • Registration Data: When you create an account, we collect the information you provide, such as your username, email address, and gender.
  • Password: We use the Firebase Authentication system for your authorization. Your password is encrypted, and we do not have direct access to it.

b) Content You Create Within the App:

  • Video and Audio Stream: The App uses your device's camera and microphone to make video calls. This data is transmitted in real-time, and we do not store it on our servers.
  • Text Messages: Messages you send are stored temporarily to be delivered to the recipient during the conversation. After the conversation ends, the chat history is deleted.

c) Third-Party Services:

We use third-party services that may have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database): Used for user authentication, account management, and database functionality. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Agora.io (RTC SDK): Used for real-time video and audio transmission. For details, see Agora's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the App's services: creating your account, connecting you with a partner, and enabling video calls.
  • Communication: To contact you with service-related notices if necessary.
  • Security: To protect our services and detect potential fraud.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With Other Users: Your username and video/audio/text content are shared with the user you are talking to.
  • By Law: If required by law or a court order.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and its associated data. To do so, you can contact us at the email address provided below.
