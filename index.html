Privacy Policy for LoveConnect

Last Updated: June 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how LoveConnect ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By creating an account and using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, password, and gender. We also require you to confirm you are 18 years of age or older.
  • Block List: When you block another user, we store this information to prevent future connections between you and the blocked user.

b) Content You Create in the App:

  • Video and Audio Stream: The App uses your device's camera and microphone for live video calls. This data is streamed in real-time and is not stored by us.
  • Text Messages: Messages you send during a call are stored temporarily for delivery and are deleted after the call ends.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's services, including creating your account, matching you with other users, and enabling video calls.
  • To enforce our rules and ensure a safe environment by using your block list to prevent unwanted interactions.
  • To manage your account, including handling account deletion requests.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it only in the following cases:

  • With Other Users: Your username and video/audio/text content are shared in real-time with the user you are connected to.
  • With Service Providers: We use third-party services like Google Firebase (for authentication and database) and Agora.io (for video streaming). These services have their own privacy policies.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users and the public.

4. Your Rights and Choices

a) Managing Your Block List:

You can view and manage your list of blocked users from the "Blocked" section within the app, accessible from the main filter screen.

b) Account Deletion:

You have the right to permanently delete your account at any time. This action is irreversible and will remove all your associated data from our systems.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18.
